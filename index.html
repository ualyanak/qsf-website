Our Story

Quantum Strategy Fund was founded by a team of physicists and mathematicians right before the 2020 pandemic escalated. We recognized that medium-term market behavior often defies explanation by classical models, and is best suited for quantum mechanics. Short-term price action is typically driven by high-frequency trading algorithms, while long-term trends tend to follow macroeconomic forces. But neither of these frameworks effectively captures the medium-term dynamics, which are heavily influenced by retail investor behavior. We developed a new framework grounded in quantum mechanics, designed specifically to navigate and predict medium-term asset pricing.

In order to meet the needs of different investors, we developed three distinct tiers:

  • Medium Risk — For stabler growth with controlled drawdowns
  • High Risk — For aggressive growth with active rebalancing
  • Extreme Risk — Designed for maximum returns

Over the past five years, that framework has delivered results. Our fund has achieved strong performance by applying quantum-native models to signal detection, portfolio optimization, and risk analysis. View our performance tracker for the 5-year cumulative return for each tier.

The High Risk tier came to fruition in August 2023, and the Extreme Risk tier became available to investors in December 2024. We are constantly improving our models and actively testing new methods to meet the needs of investors with diverse interests and risk profiles.

Why Quantum?

Quantum Strategy Fund was built on a genuine love for quantum mechanics and a belief that its principles can change the way we understand markets. This is not a conventional fund built only to generate returns. It is also a platform for curiosity, learning, and discovery.

Medium-term market behavior is one of the hardest horizons to model. Short-term moves are often dominated by high-frequency trading, while long-term trends tend to follow macroeconomic forces. The middle ground is shaped by retail sentiment, bursts of collective behavior, and rapid shifts in risk appetite. These forces are too unpredictable for most classical models, which often smooth over the very details that matter.

Quantum mechanics gives us tools designed for uncertainty. Instead of assuming one fixed outcome, quantum-inspired models account for multiple possibilities at once, allowing us to simulate overlapping scenarios and capture the complex feedback loops that drive retail-driven markets. The result is a framework that adapts in real time and responds to the subtle signals that traditional approaches miss.

Our most advanced models remain proprietary, but we believe knowledge should not be locked away. We create opportunities for investors to learn the fundamentals of quantum thinking and to see how those ideas can be applied to finance. Through workshops, resources, and direct conversations, we aim to give every investor the tools to think differently about risk, opportunity, and market behavior.
